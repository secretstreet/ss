An Escape from the Ordinary...

Here at Secret Street, our mission is to provide you with an amazing experience. Our activities are nothing but extraordinary and will entertain, promote teamwork, and engage your mind. We offer activities for groups up to 12 people. Our escape rooms range from small, 2-6 people sized rooms, where you have one hour, and larger, 6-12 people sized rooms, where we set the room slightly differently (to make it more challenging) and give you 1 and a half hours.



FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)


Q: What is an escape room?
A: An escape room is a room where you and your group are faced with a series of challenges and puzzles and where your end goal is to escape.

Q: Is there an age requirement or limit?
A: We do not have any age requirements or limits, but we do have some recommendations. To make the experience more enjoyable, we recommend that you are 10 or older. In addition, there needs to be at least one person that's 18 or older in each party.

Q: Is there parking on site?
A: Yes.

Q: When should my group and I arrive?
A: The best time to arrive is 15 minutes before your scheduled time.

Q: What if my party is not 6 or 12 people?
A: You can decide to just stay with your group, or you can let other guests join your party.

Q: How do I sign up? Should I sign up in advance?
A: You can sign up on our website by clicking the link on our navigation bar. We strongly recommend signing up online in advance as our rooms are often reserved and full.

Q: What if we can't break out?
A: Don't worry! All of the people who did not break out of our escape rooms still had lots of fun! In addition we will be monitoring your progress and will give you hints if you ask for them.

Q: Will you really lock us in?
A: We do not really lock our rooms so in case of an emergency you can escape or if you need to use the restroom, you can.

Q: How much does it cost?
A: Every single room costs $12 for each minor and $14 for each adult.
