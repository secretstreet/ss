Our Rooms



Kidnapped

Someone has kidnapped you and your team and you need to escape before your kidnapper comes back from breakfast. In this room, you will be faced with an intense, exciting situation. You will start out in a hotel room, handcuffed, and will have to make it out of the hotel.

According to Legends, the Island of _________ has a hidden tresure that is worth fortunes. The Island has been narrowed down to the Bermuda triangle. Unfortunatly you have been on vacation in Bermuda with your friends and the ship has sunk. You survive to find a hidden island ...




Castle Siege

This unique escape room has you in a castle with your team. Invaders are attacking and they will break in in an hour. Lead your team through dungeons and towers to escape. Perchance you may not escape...







Star Wars VR Escape Room



Choose between the light and the dark side as you and your team have to escape from prison. Use your brain as you have to use different tools and weapons as you try to escape from the enemy side. Keep in mind there are enemy soldiers. May the force be with you...
